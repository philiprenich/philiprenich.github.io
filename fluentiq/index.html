Let's test your English!

FluentIQ tests your English conversation skills. the test takes about 10 to 15 minutes and is split into 4 areas:

  • Speech Sounds

    Record yourself reaading aloud some short sentences.

  • Listening Accuracy

    Listen to audio clips of people speaking and type what you hear.

  • Conversation Fluency

    Record yourself speaking a short paragraph.

  • Understanding Meaning

    Listen to audio clips of people speaking and answer some multiple choice questions.
