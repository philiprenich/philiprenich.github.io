No sound was recorded?

The microphone on your computer or headset may be muted. If you are using a headset check the mute button is not activated. If you are using the microphone on your computer or webcam then check the microphone settings in your system preferences.

Sound recording is poor quality or noisy?

This is usually due to recording in a noisy place or because your sound card is not recording very well.

  1. Try finding a quieter place and retry.

  2. Try using the Chrome browser on your Android phone if you have one (sorry iPhone is not supported yet).
